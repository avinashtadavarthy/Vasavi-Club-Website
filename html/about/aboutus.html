/ About / About Vasavi Club 2* KCGF Elite

Vasavi Club 2* KCGF Elite Chennai is a subsidiary of Vasavi Clubs International running in Chennai City, Tamil Nadu, India.
It was established in 2009.
Vanitha Club Elite Chennai runs hand-in-hand with the Vasavi Club counterpart and majorly caters to the women members in the club.
It has a total of 60 couples as of 2020.
