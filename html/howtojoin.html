/ How to Join

CLUB MEMBER ELIGIBILITY AND DUTIES

  • Club Members must belong to Arya Vysya only and attain the minimum age of 18 years.
  • The Members should belong to any of 102 gothras.
  • Member must submit his/her details in Membership Form No.1006.
  • Member must take the Oath at the time of joining the club.
  • Member must obey the VCI rules and regulations of VIPPRO.
  • Member must pay the prescribed Annual Activity Fund in time.
  • Member must attend all GB Meetings & all Invited Programmes.
  • Member must wear Vasavi Lapel Pin in all Vasavi Meetings and they can wear the same in their day to day life also.
  • Member must give his/her Time-Treasure-Talent for Development of the Club and serve the needy.
  • Member must mention Vn. (Vasavian) before his/her name in all matters related to Vasavi Clubs International.


If the above mentioned criteria are fulfilled and the duties are understood, any Arya Vysya community member can become a member of any Vasavi Club International subsidiary across the globe.
This membership is by invitation ONLY. Interested couples or individuals can contact any of the known members or clubs if they want to join.
Upon proper scrutiny of the application and on collection of the applicable membership fees, the member will be able to join the club.



For anybody who is interested to join Vasavi Club 2* KCGF Elite Chennai may get in touch with any of the members known to them (as mentioned in the website).
For any further queries, please check the 'Contact Us' page.
